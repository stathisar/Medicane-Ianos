Current repository includes various scripts, performance metrics and sample checks regarding the research published in OAJAST of OPAST publishers with title:

Exploiting multiple social media sources and multiple modalities for severe weather management: The case study of the medicane Ianos.

All the sample checks performed in the research paper, exist. They had been submitted to the journal along with the manuscript. In case you are interested to view them or other materis feel free to contact. There are privacy issues with the social media data, that's why it was decided not to have them available in public.
